How We Use Your Information

A Note About Privacy

The StudyBuddy Team believes in keep our user's information safe, secure, and anonymous as possible. We only use your email to verify your idenity as a student within the app. We promise to never send you spam, because we don't have time for that either.

We require that you sign up for StudyBuddy with your first and last name, an email, and the school that you attend. In the app, we only display your name on posts and comments you make. We don't have profiles because we believe you don't need to see someone's profile to decide if they'd make a good StudyBuddy. Perserving anonmity as much as possible allows you to find new people opportunities without worrying too much about the finding.

Basically, we keep it simple and safe. Information we collect and display is just enough for what you need to get you a good StudyBuddy. Happy Studying!
