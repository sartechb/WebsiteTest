The Team


Be Flexible.

Wobetto is available on any platform where a web browser is available. In the future we plan to launch dedicated mobile apps. Creating an account takes only a minute, and we’ll keep you updated on any new features to make your studying experiences as simple as possible!



Keep it Personal.

Need to find your class information ASAP, but don’t want to search through different conversation threads? Have no fear. You can search easier by simply using our class filters. Keep the clutter in your dorm room, not in your courses.



Instant Collaboration.

Each thread has a focused class and assignment. Once you’ve found your peers, you can decide on the best location to meet and study. We help you start the conversation online, allowing your peers to become more accessible and communicating in person is easier.



Working Smarter.

Let’s defeat the passive process of reading and rereading a textbook. Studies show that working in small groups, or “cooperative learning” allows students to invent new ways of learning and understanding concepts that otherwise would be lost in a lecture. Our app is designed so you can work outside of your hastily scribbled notes.



Build a Network.

New classes? New peers? Each semester is a different challenge, but no one should go through it alone. Our app allows you to meet new people and become exposed to fresh ideas and perspectives. The teamwork skills you build now can reflect in your ability to collaborate with coworkers in the real-world. Together we can turn stress into success.
