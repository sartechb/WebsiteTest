How We Use Your Information

A Note About Privacy

The Wobetto Team believes in keep our user's information safe, secure, and anonymous as possible. We only use your email to verify your idenity as a student within the app. We promise to never send you spam, because we don't have time for that either.

We require that you sign up for Wobetto with your first and last name, an email, and the school that you attend. In the app, we only display your name on posts and comments you make.

Basically, we keep it simple and safe. Information we collect and display is just enough for what you need find other students and collaborate. Happy Studying!
