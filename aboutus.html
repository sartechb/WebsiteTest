A Message From the Wobetto Team

Hey!

We’re the Wobetto team, and our mission is to help you, the student, work better together and more efficiently.

Science has proven that people work better when exposed to different perspectives and when they have someone to help them out, so we decided to take that science and apply it to social media. We help you connect online, so you can be more effective offline.

We’re a group of students from Michigan, trying to make a difference in the way we learn with technology at our side. There are so many possibilities, yet we felt like people had explored so few! We knew we had the opportunity to do something great, and we wanted to make an impact within our community.

We’re so grateful we’ve gotten this far-from our humble dorm room and coffee shop beginnings to your computer screen, the Wobetto team have put together our best ideas and hardest work to help you Work Better Together.

We’re excited to see how you use our app and we hope that you’ll have as much fun using it as we had creating it.

-The Wobetto Team
